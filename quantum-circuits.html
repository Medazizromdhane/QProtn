Understanding Quantum Circuits

Quantum circuits are essential components in quantum computing, allowing us to perform complex computations leveraging quantum properties such as superposition and entanglement.

Key concepts include:

  • Qubits: Quantum bits representing 0, 1, or both simultaneously due to superposition.
  • Quantum Gates: Operations manipulating qubits (e.g., Hadamard, CNOT) for computation.
  • Quantum Algorithms: Utilizing quantum circuits to solve problems faster than classical computers.
